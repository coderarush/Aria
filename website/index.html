FAQ

Good questions.

Is it really free to run?

Yes — you pay once for the app. It runs on the free tiers of multiple AI providers and rotates across them, so you never pay for usage. Each provider key is free to create.

What does “controls any app” actually mean?

Aria reads what's on your screen and can click, type, and run menus in any Mac app — by voice. For apps it can't read directly, it looks at the screen with vision. It always asks before destructive actions and shows a visible “Aria is controlling your Mac” indicator you can Stop.

Is my data private?

Aria runs locally on your Mac. Wake-word detection and your optional voiceprint stay on-device. Only the request you make goes to the AI provider you've chosen.

Do I need to be technical?

No. Install it, grant microphone + accessibility access, paste a free API key, and start talking.
