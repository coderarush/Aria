It works your apps for you

Aria reads what's on screen and clicks buttons, fills fields, and runs menus in any Mac app, by voice, even apps with no way to script them. It asks before anything irreversible, and shows a marker while it's at the controls so you can stop it. No other voice assistant does this.

·

Multi-step tasks

Give a goal; it plans the steps, runs them with a crew of agents, checks the result, recovers from snags, and tells you what it did.

·

Real conversation

Say “Hey Aria,” then talk. Cut in mid-sentence and it stops to listen. A natural cloud voice, not a robot.

·

It remembers you

“Remember that…” and it keeps the fact across days. Your preferences carry over instead of resetting each session.

·

Your calendar and mail

Add events, set reminders, draft replies, take notes, play music. Spoken, hands free.

Stays on your Mac

Wake-word listening and your voiceprint never leave the machine. Only the request you make is sent out.

Asks before it deletes

Questions

Questions
Is it really free to run?+

Yes. You pay once for the app. It runs on the free tiers of several AI providers and rotates across them, so you never pay per use. Each provider key is free to create.

What does “works your apps” mean?+

Aria reads the controls on your screen and can click, type, and run menus in any Mac app, spoken. For apps it can't read directly it looks at the screen with vision. It confirms before destructive actions and shows a marker you can stop.

Where does my data go?+

Aria runs on your Mac. Wake-word detection and your optional voiceprint stay on-device. Only the request you make goes to the provider you chose.

Do I need to be technical?+

No. Install it, grant microphone and accessibility access, paste a free API key, and start talking.
