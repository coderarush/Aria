Questions

Is it really free to run?

Yes. You pay once for the app. It runs on free AI provider tiers and rotates across them, so you never pay for usage. Add your own keys (all free to create) and it just works.

What does “controls any app” mean?

Aria reads what's on your screen and can click buttons, fill fields, and run menus in any Mac app — by voice. It asks before anything destructive, and shows a visible indicator while it's in control.

Is my data private?

Aria runs locally on your Mac. Wake-word listening and your voiceprint stay on-device. Only the request you make is sent to the AI provider you chose.

Do I need to be technical?

No. Install, grant mic + accessibility access, paste a free API key, and talk to it.
